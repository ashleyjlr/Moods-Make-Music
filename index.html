Moods Make Music

sometimes we can't find the words to express how we feel, so let us find the songs to do just that.

Mental health impacts almost every aspect of our lives, which is why it is important to keep up with our moods. Although there are endless complex emotions we feel in our day to day lives, they all ultimately fall under these categories: Happy, Sad, Angry, or Tired. Many researchers claim that music has the ability to boost moods in addition to many other stress-alleviating benefits. Even sad music can lift your mood! So sit back, enjoy, and let the music take control <3

Moods and You

hap·py /ˈhapē/ feeling or showing pleasure or contentment: "They danced to the exciting and happy beat."

happy → when you’re feeling happy, why not have a dance party in your room to new music? celebrate the serotonin!

sad /sad/ feeling or showing sorrow; unhappy: "I was sad and subdued listening to Bruno Mars."

sad → feeling sad? it’s okay, cry it all out. music is here for you. listen to some sad tunes to feel better!

mad /mad/ very angry: “She was mad that he took the aux.”

mad → need to let out some anger? feel free to jump up & down and scream out the lyrics to these catchy songs! know your worth like the bad bleep you are!

sleep·y /ˈslēpē/ needing or ready for sleep: "The music had made her sleepy."

sleepy → it must be tiring to be as amazing as you. let the music calm you down. :)
